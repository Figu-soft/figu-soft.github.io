Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Flappy Meowl is a free casual game designed for entertainment purposes.

Flappy Meowl does not collect or store personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

This game contains advertisements provided by third-party ad networks, including Google AdMob. These services may use the device’s Advertising ID and other non-personal data to display relevant ads and measure ad performance.

The Advertising ID may be used for personalized advertising purposes. Users can reset or limit ad personalization through their device settings.

3. Third-Party Services

Flappy Meowl integrates third-party services for advertising. These services may collect anonymized usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage users to review Google’s Privacy Policy for more information on how advertising data is handled.

4. Offline Use

The game can be played offline. However, an internet connection may be required to display advertisements.

5. App Permissions

The application only requests permissions necessary for its basic functionality and advertising services. It does not access sensitive personal data.

6. Local Storage

The game may store data locally on your device, such as high scores, to enhance your gameplay experience. This information is not transmitted to external servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Flappy Meowl is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
